How it works

Want to Submit a Question?

Get involved and take advantage of the experience of older students by asking any academic, career or extra-curricular related questions, and having them answered in the next edition of our newsletter.

Why should you read?

• Stay up to date on school news
• Students will become educated about what's going on out side their curriculum
• Parents may even see their child featured!

How to pick up a copy:

1. Come into school,

2. Take a copy from either reception or Student Services.

3. Have a read!


Or simply visit the website! We'll have all our newsletters listed under the 'Newsletters' tab.
